Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Where are sessions held?

Sessions are available at my studio base in Basingstoke and on location throughout Hampshire and the surrounding counties, including Winchester, Andover, Farnborough, Newbury, and Reading.

How long does a session take?

Most portrait sessions last between one and two hours, depending on the type of session and the number of people involved. There is never any rush — the session ends when we have what we came for.

How soon will I see my portraits?

Your viewing appointment is typically arranged within two weeks of your session. You will see fully finished, retouched portraits — not raw previews.

What should I wear?

This is one of the things we discuss during your pre-session consultation. There are no strict rules, but guidance on colours, styles, and what tends to photograph well will be provided based on your specific session.

Do you photograph children and families?

Yes. Family sessions and portraits of children are a regular part of the work. Sessions are relaxed and paced to suit younger subjects, with plenty of room for natural moments.
