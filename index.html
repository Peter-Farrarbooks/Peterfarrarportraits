Portrait Photography

Every portrait tells a story only you can tell

Based in Basingstoke and serving Hampshire and the surrounding counties, I create classic studio and location portraits for individuals, families, performers and businesses. Whether you are looking for professional headshots in Basingstoke, personal branding photography, or a timeless family portrait, every session is tailored entirely to you.

"A portrait is not made in the camera but on either side of it." — Edward Steichen

With decades of experience photographing people from all walks of life across the UK and USA, my approach is unhurried, personal and focused entirely on producing images you will be proud to display for years to come.

As a Basingstoke portrait photographer, my work is rooted in creating natural, honest images that reflect genuine character. Portrait photography in Basingstoke, Hampshire has been at the heart of my work since returning to the UK, and every session, whether in the studio or on location, is approached with the same care and attention to detail.

Every session begins with a conversation, understanding what you want to take away, how you want to feel in front of the camera, and what story your portraits should tell. For professionals and business owners, that might mean confident headshots that reflect your personal brand. For families, it might mean relaxed, natural images that capture how you truly are together.

Whether you come to the studio or prefer a location that means something to you, Hampshire countryside, an urban setting, or somewhere with personal significance, the result is the same: natural, honest portraits with genuine character.

Sessions are available throughout Basingstoke, Winchester, Andover, Farnborough, Newbury, Reading and the wider Hampshire area, offering both studio portraits and location portraits across Hampshire. Get in touch to discuss your portrait session.
