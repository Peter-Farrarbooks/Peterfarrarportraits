Peter Farrar Updates

After some time away, I am delighted to welcome you to Peter Farrar Portraits, the new home for my photography, creative projects, and photographic journey.

Many of you will have noticed that Kenneth Light Studios quietly disappeared some time ago. Rather than simply replacing it, I wanted to create something that better reflects who I am as a photographer today and where I want to take my work in the future.

Peter Farrar Portraits brings together my passion for portrait photography, visual storytelling, and the art of creating images that capture personality, character, and emotion. It will also serve as a place to share photography articles, behind-the-scenes insights, photographic experiences, and practical tips for photographers of all levels.

You may also have noticed changes across my social media platforms. Instagram, X, Threads, and Facebook now all carry the Peter Farrar Portraits name, creating a consistent identity wherever you choose to follow my work.

While the website is now live, this is only the beginning. Over the coming weeks and months I will continue refining the site, expanding the photography galleries, publishing new blog posts, and sharing fresh content as the project develops.

Whether you are here because you enjoy portrait photography, are interested in photography techniques, or simply want to follow the next stage of my creative journey, I am grateful to have you here.

Thank you for your continued support and encouragement over the years. I look forward to sharing much more with you as Peter Farrar Portraits grows.

Welcome, and watch this space for future updates.
