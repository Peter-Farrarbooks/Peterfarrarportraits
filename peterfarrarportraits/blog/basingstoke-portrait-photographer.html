Basingstoke Portrait Photographer

My work focuses on simplicity, clarity and genuine expression, whether I am photographing in the studio or on location. After many years working under the name Kenneth Light Studios, I rebranded to Peter Farrar Portraits to bring my identity and my photography together under one consistent name across Facebook, Instagram, Threads, X and LinkedIn.

My portrait sessions are calm and unhurried, giving you the space to feel comfortable in front of the camera. Whether you need a professional headshot, a family portrait, a personal branding session or a creative studio portrait, I create images that feel authentic and timeless. I work throughout Basingstoke and the surrounding Hampshire areas, offering both studio portraits and location photography depending on the style you prefer.

If you are looking for a portrait photographer in Basingstoke who values authenticity, simplicity and a relaxed experience, I would be happy to work with you. Every session is tailored to you, and every portrait is created with care and attention.
