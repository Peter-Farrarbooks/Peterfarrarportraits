The Photographer

About Peter Farrar Portrait Photographer in Basingstoke

Portrait Photographer · Author · Mentor

Photography has been a lifelong passion and profession that has taken me across continents, introduced me to remarkable people, and allowed me to capture some of life's most important moments.

Professional Portrait Photographer in Basingstoke

Over the years I have worked as a professional photographer in both the United States and the United Kingdom, specialising in portraits, weddings, special events, and commercial assignments. My career has provided opportunities to photograph people from all walks of life, helping individuals, families, professionals, performers, and businesses preserve memories and tell their stories through photography.

Today my primary focus is portrait photography in Hampshire and Basingstoke, creating professional portraits that capture personality, character, and genuine emotion. Whether working in a studio environment or on location, I strive to create images that feel natural, authentic, and timeless.

Experience Across the UK and USA

My photography career began in the United States, where I founded Kenneth Light Studios in Charlotte, North Carolina in 2008. Over the following years I built the business into a full-time professional photography studio, photographing weddings, portraits, commercial assignments and events across North Carolina and beyond.

After relocating to the United Kingdom in 2014, I continued to develop my skills and client base, photographing people across Hampshire and the South of England. In 2026, I rebranded to Peter Farrar Portraits to bring my name and my photography together under one consistent identity, the same approach, the same standard, the same commitment to creating portraits that last.

My career has never been limited to photography alone. Throughout the years I have also worked on video and film projects for clients, gaining valuable experience in visual storytelling, lighting, composition, and creative direction. These skills continue to influence my photographic work today, helping me create images that go beyond simply recording a moment.

A People-Focused Approach to Portrait Photography

Portrait photography is not just about technical ability, it is about understanding people. Every successful portrait begins with communication, trust, and creating an environment where clients feel comfortable and confident in front of the camera.

My sessions are unhurried by design. There is always time to settle in, try different approaches, and find the images that genuinely reflect who you are. I do not believe in rushing people or pushing for a result before the subject is ready. The best portraits come when people forget about the camera, and that takes time, patience, and the right atmosphere.

This approach has helped me build lasting relationships with clients and produce portraits that reflect who they truly are, not just how they look.

Published Author and Photography Educator

In addition to my work behind the camera, I am a published author with books covering both photography and leadership. Peter Farrar Books covers the full range of my writing work, including the Mastering Digital Photography three-volume series and The Complete Photography Field Guide — a 538-page reference covering technique, workflow, business, and UK and US photography law in a single volume.

Teaching and mentoring others has always been important to me. I have trained over 300 photographers throughout my career, sharing the techniques, approaches, and practical knowledge that come from decades of professional work. Whether helping aspiring photographers develop their skills or sharing leadership principles from my corporate career, I enjoy encouraging others to grow.

Creative Influences

Creativity does not end when the camera is put away. I have a lifelong appreciation for music, film, and visual storytelling. Many of the principles that make powerful cinema resonate with audiences, composition, emotion, timing, and narrative, can also be found in strong portrait photography. Every portrait is a story, and finding that story is what drives my work.

Travel has played an important role throughout my life as well. Experiencing different places, cultures, and perspectives has shaped both my personal outlook and my creative vision. From the mountains of North Carolina to the Hampshire countryside, every environment offers new inspiration and a deeper appreciation for the diversity of people and the stories they carry.

Portrait Photography Across Hampshire and Basingstoke

Based in Basingstoke, I work with clients throughout Hampshire and the surrounding counties. Studio sessions take place in Basingstoke, offering full control of lighting and environment. Location sessions are available anywhere across Hampshire, from the countryside and villages of the Test Valley and Itchen Valley to urban settings in Winchester, Reading, Newbury and Farnborough.

Whether you are looking for a professional headshot, a relaxed family portrait, personal branding photography, or a creative commission, my goal is the same: to create images that you will be proud to display and treasure for years to come.

Thank you for taking the time to visit. I look forward to meeting you and helping tell your story through photography.
